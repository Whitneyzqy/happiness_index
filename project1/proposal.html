Correlation Between World Happiness Index and Economics

Problem/Data Domain

Along with the development of society, people now pay more attention to their quality of life. Generally, their levels of satisfaction can be reflected with happiness scores, which may be related to various factors such as economy, health, or other social factors. Our group selected the data of happiness indexes from different regions based on the World Happiness Report.

The problem we are trying to solve is to figure out the correlation between economics, health, trust in government, level of freedom and their happiness indices within different countries from 2015 to 2019. Through analyzing these datasets, we will provide users who want to immigrate or travel to another country with statistical data and visualizations. These visualizations will provide objective information to help them make decisions.

Previously, US News set up a website called “Best Countries for Immigrants” to provide information for the user. They have a large set of statistical datasets, but no visualizations or relevant data analysis is involved. Thus the goal of this project is to analyze and visualize these datasets to provide a more direct insight for the users.

App Description

Who are the users?

Users of this web application can be the researchers or government workers who study how happiness may be affected by other social factors, or immigrants who want to know about the country they move to. With the filtered data, they can browse on some specific relationships between happiness and each component.

What can users see?

The user will be able to see the dataset containing research data of happiness index on Kaggle, and economics of different countries on World Bank Dsata. In addition to the raw data, we will provide users with visualizations to understand the data more directly.

What can users interaction with?

The user can customize how they want to filter the data by different factors, such as economics, health, or time. Different visualizations or dataset will show up based on the users’ desires. The users can also use forms to search for specific data within the dataset which they can use it to provide their own research in the future.

How can this app solve the problem?

Through these visualizations and datasets, users will gain a better acknowledgment of the correlation between happiness indices and different factors of the country, so that they can publish their studies or take actions accordingly.
